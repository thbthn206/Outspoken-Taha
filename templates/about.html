About Me

This is what I do.

My name is Taha and I'm a Turkish person. I came into the world on August 13, 2000, at 5:30 in the morning. However, the specifics of when my life will come to an end remain unknown. While I have insights into certain aspects of the time that spans from my birth to an eventual demise, I anticipate discovering more about this period as time unfolds.

During the initial phase of my life, I was an outspoken individual, often laughing freely and expressing my thoughts without reservation. However, as time passed, I transformed into an introverted person, given to contemplation. Presently, my smiles convey the disposition of a cheerful pessimist.

Recently, I've been feeling a longing for my previous straightforwardness. I desire to openly share the outcomes of my continuous contemplation, unveiling aspects of my thoughts without reservation. My intention is that through sharing my readings, ideas, emotions, and more, I can evolve into a cheerful yet mature, optimistic yet realistic individual. This website, aptly named "Outspoken Taha" serves as a platform for this purpose.
